## Key metrics (top row)

## Key findings

- Gini at all-time high (30.6 in 2023)
- Top 1% wealth grew 22% in 3 years vs 8% for median
- D10 income doubled since 1987, D1 only +44%
- Gentofte Gini (54.2) is 2.3× Halsnæs (23.6)
- 6 billion DKK could fund 143% of short-cycle higher education or 89% of preventive healthcare

## Data caveats

- Wealth data (formue16) only covers 2020–2023 (4 years)
- The 6 billion DKK revenue estimate is a political projection, not a DST calculation
- Gini is based on equivalized disposable income, not wealth
